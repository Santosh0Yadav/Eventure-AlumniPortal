DGI Eventure

The Eventure is a platform designed for the alumni of the college to connect with each other, share their experiences and memories, and stay informed about the latest news and events at the college. Through this portal, alumni can access exclusive content, participate in alumni events, and stay in touch with their alma mater. It serves as a virtual home for alumni, fostering a sense of belonging and community while enabling them to maintain strong ties with their peers and the institution. Eventure is more than just a networking tool; it is a space where alumni can celebrate their achievements, engage in meaningful discussions, and contribute to the college's ongoing development.
